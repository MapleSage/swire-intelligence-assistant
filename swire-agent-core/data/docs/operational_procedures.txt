Swire Renewables Operational Procedures

Site Operations:
- Daily site inspections at 0800 hours
- Equipment status reports submitted by 1000 hours
- Maintenance schedules reviewed weekly
- Performance metrics tracked hourly

Wind Farm Management:
- Turbine availability target: 97%
- Scheduled maintenance windows: Sundays 0600-1200
- Remote monitoring 24/7 from control center
- Predictive maintenance using vibration analysis

Quality Control:
- ISO 9001:2015 compliance required
- Monthly quality audits conducted
- Corrective action plans within 48 hours
- Continuous improvement initiatives

Environmental Compliance:
- Wildlife monitoring during migration seasons
- Noise level measurements quarterly
- Soil and water testing annually
- Carbon footprint reporting monthly
