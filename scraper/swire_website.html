Providing reliable inspection, repair, and maintenance solutions

Swire Renewable Energy operates across Europe, the UK, Africa, and Asia Pacific, providing expert services to support wind turbine manufacturers and operators. Our comprehensive offering includes inspection, construction, installation, blade services, electrical and HV expertise, and more. Whether onshore or offshore, we are dedicated to enhancing the performance and longevity of renewable energy assets.
